How to get started with social media marketing in 2020?

Posted by Rishikesh Jha • June 1st, 2020

Hey! So finally, you figured it out that social media marketing can do wonders to your business or you are just starting as a social media marketer and helping your client scale his business.

Here are 6 steps to get started with the social media marketing.

1.Know your goal

It is good to figure out what specifically you want to achieve from your campaign. whether it’s an ad campaign that you want to run, or you want to get direct sales. Whether you want visitors on your website or you want your newly developed app to get downloaded for the customer’s convenience. Once, you have figured it out, you will know what sort of social media platform you are gonna use and audience you want to focus.

2.Know where you want to be

Once you understand the goal of your social media marketing campaign, you need to select the social media platforms wisely according to the nature of your business and the audience that you are targeting. Selecting a social media platform also depends on the kind of content that you are producing. So, if you are a B2B business and you can produce video contents, you should choose YouTube or LinkedIn. But if you are a B2C, you can choose Facebook or Instagram and also YouTube depending upon your target audience. Only be on the platforms that seems relevant and always prioritise quality over quantity.

3.Create amazing content

Once you have completed the profile on any social media, you should start researching on the content. Create quality content that attracts audience. That could be a video, a short movie, a graphical post or an animated video. Never shy away from trying new things as they can turn out to be most traffic generating content and it is because any trend that content creators follow does not last forever, so your audience like to see new stuff popping up in their feed.

4.Know about your competition

Once your content starts getting traffic, you should research about your competitors who might be focusing on the same sort of things as you are having. So, it’s better to keep evolving with both your content and your presence online and your services on the ground. Look what you can do to add value to your audience that your competitor is not doing.

5.Use your marketing skills in the campaign

As you are adding value to the people who are consuming your content and seeing your brand’s posts in their feed, you can share links that is related to direct or indirect sales or that can lead to subscription. You can ask them for visiting your website or subscribe to your services. Use your marketing mind and create a loyal audience base so that you can promote your services both online and offline and across other platforms.

6.Create Engagement

Algorithm of the social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram promotes the posts that is more engaging and it is measured in the sense of how many comments a particular post has. So, work on your caption alongwith your content so that, it can compel audience to comment. Also, you should reply on their comments and sometimes just let them interact among themselves so that they can build an emotional value with your post and your brand.
