5 best way to learn mobile App Development?

Posted by Arijeet Mukherjee • June 1st, 2020

Getting started with programming language might be a confusing one but a proper guide can let you in. Mobile App development is one such cutting edge technology neccesity in todays world. Lets understand simple steps to start with mobile app development.

Here are 5 steps to get started with the Mobile App Development.

1.Know your goal

Initially try getting away from any form of machinery or technology and grap a pen and some papers and jord down what you actually want to achieve? Any form of development firstly requires a paper work, not complicated coding and designing. And then Ask and answer the following questions:

  • What do you want your app to do or what will be its basic functionality?
  • How does your application arouse a demand in the market or can catch user attentions?
  • What problems you want your app to solve in real life?
  • How will the user gets benifit of it?
  • Your buisness will not succeed unless a proper goal is set! A inadequacy of view will frustrate you and can break you in, anyone who you employ to work for you can even loose confident in you. Before you do anything, create a bright rising picture of what you want done.

2.Make Research, do Research and only get doing Research

Now that you have your goal set, can now turn your computer on, with google in action but yes not to start aimlessly designing your app. The leg work is nowhere near done. You have to get in digging broad and research the competitors of your app idea. I know you worked and think hard setting your ideas, but I afraid the numbers may be favor-odds as someone has already tried it. You can look at this situation like a two-face coin and can be in dilema. Firstly you can become deflated, morose and give up, or secondly, you can examine the competition and make your app better. I prefer not giving in untill tried. Read the competitionâ€™s revision. Note down What did people like/dislike about the app? Then, use that information to your influence and implement to overcome the people's dislike. Get your idea authorize by refering to the step one and two.

3.Budgeting you App

Money is a very valuable resource and we all want things done in minimum investment. Similarly an App development requires various phases of development starting from UI/UX to backending your app. Before getting into development I will suggest you to have a proper budget if you want it last long.

4.Backend your app

Okay after all the nerd work done we are good enough to dig into the main part of development. Its functionality. It is tie to set the functions the app going to perform. Lets know how? Firstly decide whether or not your app require a database. If require make one and set its API with proper endpoints. Next get ready to think about servers, API's and data diagram. There are some extreme do-it-yourself maker that can provide you with the tools to comfortably do this. Some of them even do it if for want him for you. It is you who will decide all this. Regardless of what method you choose to use to develop your app, it is compulsory that clear diagrams are created as they will serve as the directions for everyone working on your project. Should you run across any technical complication, you should revise your wireframe to reflect any changes.

5.Identifying platform and Release your App

Oh great! Having an app ready with you it is high time to get your app infront of world for marketting. Yes you are on the finish line before your app gets released. World offers you two great platform for app marketting Android and IOS. Both having its different set of rules for publications and stores where you can deploy your app. While Android has play store with less strict rules, IOS offer you App store with strictness being moderately high. So it is a challenge for you to decide which platform to opt for, ya ofcourse you can opt for both but yes how better you adop the Terms & Conditions will decide your getting published or not.
